# ChikuMiku LearnVerse — User Guide

## Welcome

ChikuMiku LearnVerse is a learning platform designed for children to study any subject — languages (Kannada, Hindi, English), Maths, Science, Computers, and more — using their own textbook content. Simply take photos of your textbook pages, and the app helps you learn through pronunciation practice, grammar exercises, comprehension questions, and revision tests.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open ChikuMiku LearnVerse on your Android device or web browser
2. Tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address or phone number
4. Create a password (8 or more characters, must include at least one letter and one number)
5. Select your current grade (1-12)
6. Choose a display name
7. Tap **Create Account**

If you make a mistake, the app will highlight which field needs fixing without clearing your other entries.

### Logging In

1. Enter your email/phone and password
2. Tap **Log In**
3. Your session stays active for at least 30 days — no need to log in every time

**Note**: After 3 incorrect password attempts, your account will be locked for 15 minutes for security. Your parent/guardian will be notified.

### Linking a Parent Account

A parent can link their account to monitor your progress:
- View learning progress across all subjects
- Reset your password if forgotten
- Update your grade and contact information

## Enrolling in Subjects

You can study up to 10 subjects at the same time. Each subject has its own separate space for content and progress.

1. From the main screen, tap **Add Subject**
2. Choose from available subjects (Kannada, Hindi, English, Maths, Science, Computers, etc.)
3. Your new subject appears in the subject selector

Switch between subjects anytime using the subject selector at the top of the screen.

## Adding Textbook Content

### Uploading Pages

1. Select your subject
2. Tap **Add Chapter**
3. Choose your textbook and chapter number
4. Upload photos of your textbook pages:
   - **Android**: Use your camera to take a photo directly
   - **Web**: Click to upload image files from your computer
5. Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, HEIC (max 10 MB per image)
6. The app extracts text from your photos automatically
7. Review the extracted text and make any corrections
8. Tap **Save** to store the chapter

### Managing Pages

- Add up to **50 pages** per chapter
- **Add more pages**: Open an existing chapter and upload additional photos
- **Reorder pages**: Drag pages to rearrange them in the correct sequence
- If text extraction fails, the app suggests corrective actions (better lighting, clearer photo)

## Learning Features

### Pronunciation Practice (Language Subjects Only)

Available for language subjects like Kannada, Hindi, and English:

1. Select a word or alphabet character from your chapter
2. Listen to the correct pronunciation (plays within 1 second)
3. Record your own attempt (up to 10 seconds)
4. Get an accuracy score (0-100%) with feedback on which syllables need work
5. See each character with its English transliteration

**Tips**:
- Make sure your microphone is enabled
- Speak clearly and at a normal volume
- If some letters show as unavailable, their audio is temporarily loading

### Grammar Exercises (Language Subjects Only)

1. Tap **Grammar Practice** within a language subject
2. Submit any sentence to check for grammatical errors
3. Get corrections with explanations appropriate for your grade level
4. Request practice exercises (5-10 per session) based on your chapter vocabulary
5. After completing exercises, see your score and which grammar rules need more practice

**Note**: You need at least one chapter saved before exercises can be generated.

### Comprehension Questions (All Subjects)

1. Upload a photo of questions from your textbook
2. The app extracts and displays the questions for you to confirm
3. Associate questions with the correct chapter
4. For each question:
   - View the model answer generated from your chapter content
   - Submit your own answer for scoring
   - Request step-by-step hints if you're stuck
5. See your performance summary: percentage score, correct/total count, and areas to improve

**Question types supported**: Fill-in-the-blanks, short answer, match-the-following, and descriptive answers.

## Revision Mode

### Starting a Revision Session

1. Tap **Revision Mode** within your subject
2. Select one, multiple, or all chapters to revise
3. The app generates 5-20 questions per chapter across different difficulty levels:
   - **Recall**: Basic facts and definitions
   - **Understanding**: Explain concepts in your own words
   - **Application**: Apply knowledge to new situations

### Timed Tests

1. In Revision Mode, enable **Timed Test**
2. Set your time limit (5 to 120 minutes)
3. Answer as many questions as you can
4. When time runs out, the test ends automatically
5. Unanswered questions are marked as unattempted
6. Your score is based only on questions you answered

### Performance Summary

After each revision session, you'll see:
- **Per-chapter scores** with percentage
- **Strengths**: Topics where you scored 70% or above
- **Areas to improve**: Topics where you scored below 70%
- Your results are saved to track improvement over time

### Saving Progress

- If you exit early, your progress is saved automatically
- You can resume the session later or view a summary of what you've answered so far

## Progress Tracking

### Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- Aggregate progress across all enrolled subjects
- Per-subject completion percentages
- Activity scores for each chapter

### Chapter Progress

For each chapter, the app tracks:
- **Completion percentage**: Proportion of available activities completed
- **Individual scores**: Per activity type (comprehension, pronunciation, grammar, revision)
- **Last accessed date**
- Activities where you scored below 60% are highlighted for focused review

## Grade Management

### Updating Your Grade

When you move to the next grade:

1. Your parent or you can update the grade in your profile
2. You'll be asked whether to **keep** or **delete** previous grade content:
   - **Keep**: Content is archived in read-only mode (you can still view it)
   - **Delete**: Requires confirmation before permanent removal
3. Progress resets for the new grade, but your cumulative achievement history is preserved

### Academic Year Notifications

- Your parent receives a notification 30 days before the configured academic year end
- They can confirm or adjust the expected promotion date

## Using the Help Button

A help button (question mark icon) is always visible in the bottom-right corner of the screen after you log in. Tap or click it at any time to open this User Guide directly inside the app.

### Navigating the Help Viewer

- A **table of contents** on the side lists all sections — tap any entry to jump directly to that section
- Scroll through the full guide content
- The active section is highlighted in the table of contents as you read

### Closing the Help Viewer

- Tap the **close button** (X) to dismiss the viewer
- On Android, press the **back button** to close
- On web, press the **Escape key** to close
- When you close the viewer, you return to exactly where you were — your scroll position, form inputs, and activity progress are all preserved

### Offline Access

- On **Android**, the User Guide is bundled with the app and always available offline
- On **Web**, the guide is cached after your first online visit — you can access it offline after that
- If you're on the web and haven't loaded the guide online yet, you'll see a message explaining that an internet connection is needed for the first load

## Using Multiple Devices

ChikuMiku LearnVerse works on:
- **Android** (8.0 and above)
- **Web browsers** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge — latest 2 versions)

### Syncing

- Your progress syncs automatically across devices within 5 seconds
- When you switch devices, the app restores exactly where you left off (subject, chapter, exercise position, and any unsaved input)
- If you edit the same content on two devices before syncing, the most recent version is kept (you can review what was overwritten)

### Offline Mode

You can keep learning even without internet:
- Previously accessed content is cached on your device (up to 500 MB)
- Actions you take offline are queued (up to 50 actions)
- Everything syncs automatically when you're back online
- If a queued action can't be saved, you'll be notified

## Local Progress Backup

Your progress is continuously backed up locally on your device:
- Backups are kept for up to 7 days
- If your session expires, simply log in again and your local progress is restored
- This works regardless of whether you're online or offline

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Good photos**: Use good lighting and hold your phone steady when photographing textbook pages
2. **Review extracted text**: Always check the extracted text before saving — fix any errors
3. **Regular revision**: Use Revision Mode regularly to identify and strengthen weak areas
4. **Multiple subjects**: Enroll in all your subjects to track everything in one place
5. **Timed tests**: Practice with timed tests before real exams to build speed
6. **Use the help button**: Tap the help icon anytime you need guidance — it's always there in the corner

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Photo text not extracting well | Retake with better lighting, ensure text is clearly visible |
| Microphone not working | Check app permissions in device settings |
| Audio not playing | Check volume, try the retry option |
| Can't save chapter | Check internet connection; content is saved locally for retry |
| Account locked | Wait 15 minutes, then try again with correct password |
| Session expired | Log in again; your local progress will be restored |
| Help guide not loading (web) | Connect to the internet once to cache the guide for offline use |

## Privacy and Safety

- Your data is private — no other learner can see your content or progress
- Parent accounts can monitor progress for child safety
- Local backups ensure you never lose work unexpectedly
